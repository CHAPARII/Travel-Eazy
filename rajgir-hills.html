About Rajgir Hills

Rajgir Hills, located in the Nalanda district of Bihar, is a blend of natural beauty and ancient history. Surrounded by lush green valleys and steep hills, it has been a favorite destination for nature enthusiasts, trekkers, and pilgrims.

Rajgir holds immense significance in Buddhist, Jain, and Hindu traditions, being a site where Buddha meditated, Mahavira preached, and ancient kings resided.

Top Attractions in Rajgir Hills

Vulture's Peak (Griddhakuta)

Vulture's Peak is a significant Buddhist site where Buddha delivered several sermons. It offers breathtaking views of the valley below.

Rajgir Ropeway

India's oldest ropeway takes you up to the Vishwa Shanti Stupa, offering a scenic journey through the hills.

Hot Springs

The hot springs in Rajgir are renowned for their therapeutic properties and attract visitors looking for relaxation and rejuvenation.

Adventures and Trekking

The Rajgir Hills are a haven for adventurers. Popular trekking routes lead to ancient caves, stupas, and hilltops, offering panoramic views of the lush landscape.

  • Vulture's Peak Trek
  • Shanti Stupa Trek
  • Cycling trails through the hills
