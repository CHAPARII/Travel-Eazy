Welcome to Sasaram

Explore the historic city of Sasaram, home to the magnificent tomb of Sher Shah Suri, and much more!

Historical Significance

Sasaram is a city with deep historical roots, famous for the tomb of Sher Shah Suri, the founder of the Suri Empire. The city was once a center of power, witnessing significant historical events in medieval India.

Sasaram is also known for its architectural wonders, particularly the **Sher Shah Suri Tomb**, which is a remarkable example of Afghan architecture.

Top Attractions in Sasaram

Sher Shah Suri Tomb

The **Sher Shah Suri Tomb** is the most iconic structure in Sasaram, built by Sher Shah's son, Islam Shah. The tomb stands on an island in a large pool and is an architectural marvel from the 16th century.

Kartoli Temple

The **Kartoli Temple** is dedicated to Lord Shiva and offers a serene and peaceful environment for spiritual reflection. It is located on the outskirts of Sasaram.
